
## Solarized

source: `git clone git@github.com:altercation/solarized.git`

### IntelliJ

1. `File | Import Settings... | intellij-colors-solarized`; Restart
-  `Preferences | Editor | Colors & Fonts`

`Preferences | Profiles | Colors | Load Presets... | import... `
